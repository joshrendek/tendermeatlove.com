---
title: "Bean Salad"
date: 2018-07-19T20:18:27-04:00
draft: false
time: 30m
servings: 6
calories: 380
source: Mom
ingredients:
    - 3 cans black beans
    - 1 cups cooked rice
    - 1-2 bunches of green onions
    - 2 ribs celery
    - 3 cloves garlic
    - 1 cup shredded cheddar
    - zesty italian dressing
    - frozen corn
    - jalapeno
    - red bell pepper
categories:
    - main dish
tags:
    - cold
    - meal prep

---

* Cook and cool rice
* Drain and rinse black beans
* Slice celery into small pieces
* Mince garlic
* Thaw/cook corn partially
* Dice bell pepper and jalapeno
* Mix everything together in bowl
* Add 1/2 bottle of Italian dressing and season with salt and pepper to taste
