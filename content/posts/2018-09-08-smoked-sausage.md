* Pre-heat smoker to 275 for 45 minutes before hand
* Lower to 225-250 when cooking
* Smoke for 2-3 hours, internal temp should be 160F
* Prep charcoal grill
* Finish/crisp up on grill for 2-5 minutes and serve
